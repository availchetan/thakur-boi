Recent Learnings

1:1 Conversations – Understanding the Underlying Cause
  • Objective: Understand why the behavior is happening.
  • Causes to Explore:
    • Burnout: Signs of fatigue or disengagement.
    • Role mismatch: Work that doesn’t leverage strengths.
    • Personal challenges: Life events impacting focus.
  • Approach: Use open-ended questions:

    “I’ve noticed some tension in recent meetings. How are things going on your end?”

    “What’s been feeling most challenging in your work lately?”

Supportive Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)
  • Set Clear Expectations:
    • Define specific, measurable goals (e.g., “Code reviews within 48 hours”).
  • Timeline: Typically 4–6 weeks with weekly progress check-ins.
  • Goal: Provide support and resources to foster improvement.
Addressing Toxic Behavior

Use the Situation-Behavior-Impact (SBI) model for feedback:

  • Situation: “In yesterday’s team meeting…”
  • Behavior: “You interrupted junior members…”
  • Impact: “It discouraged team contribution.”

Loop in HR if behavior persists despite feedback.
